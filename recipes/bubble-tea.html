Bubble Tea

Description

This recipe was not passed down by my grandmother.

I got it from ChatGPT hehe XD.

Ingredients

  • Tea bags
  • Tapioca pearls
  • Brown sugar
  • Milk
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil
  2. Add tapioca pearls and stir
  3. Turn off heat, cover, and let sit another 5 minutes
  4. Drain and mix with brown sugar
