Chicken Rice

Description

This recipe was passed down by my grandmother.

LOL jk I got it from ChatGPT hehe XD.

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Pandan leaves
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Rub the chicken with salt, then rinse
  2. Boil water then add in ginger and garlic
  3. Add chicken and poach gently
  4. Remove and immediately place in ice water
